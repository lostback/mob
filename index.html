Level 1: Ground School

Complete these essential skills before attempting any flight.

+

Key Information:

  • mAh: Battery capacity. Higher mAh means longer flight times but adds weight.
  • C-Rating: Discharge rate. Higher C-ratings give bursts of power but may wear out faster.
  • Use 520mAh for practice flights and 300mAh for quick bursts of power.
  • Always handle LiPo batteries carefully to avoid damage or hazards.
+

Key Steps:

  1. Always power on the Jumper T-Pro transmitter first.
  2. Connect the battery to the Mobula7 and place it on a flat surface for gyroscope calibration.
  3. Ensure the transmitter is bound to the drone before arming.
