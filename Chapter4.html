Intermediate Maneuvers

Build on your basic skills to improve precision, speed, and control.

+

Key Tips:

  • Use yaw and roll together to create smooth, controlled turns.
  • Practice slow, wide turns before progressing to tighter ones.
  • Focus on maintaining altitude while turning.
+

Flying through gaps improves precision:

  • Start with larger gaps, such as doorways or between cones.
  • Gradually attempt tighter spaces as your confidence grows.
  • Maintain a steady speed and small stick inputs for control.
+

Practice flying faster while staying in control:

  • Gradually increase throttle while flying forward.
  • Focus on maintaining a consistent altitude.
  • Combine speed with gentle turns to simulate racing.
+

Smooth altitude control is crucial for precision:

  • Practice slow ascents and descents using throttle control.
  • Maintain forward movement during altitude changes.
  • Combine altitude changes with turns for more dynamic practice.
+

Create a simple course with household items:

  • Use cones, chairs, or other objects as obstacles.
  • Practice flying through, over, or around them.
  • Combine turns, altitude changes, and gaps for a challenge.
+

Handle unexpected situations effectively:

  • If the drone tips or flips, disarm immediately to avoid damage.
  • Use yaw to realign orientation after losing track.
  • Land safely and reset when necessary.
