What are PID Settings?

  • P: Proportional - Controls responsiveness to inputs.
  • I: Integral - Compensates for small errors over time.
  • D: Derivative - Smooths out abrupt changes in input.

Use the Betaflight Configurator to adjust these settings. Start small and test after each change.

+

Practice flying through tighter spaces while maintaining speed:

  • Use cones, chairs, or doorways as practice obstacles.
  • Focus on maintaining a steady throttle and minimizing stick movements.
  • Gradually increase speed as your confidence improves.

A Matty Flip combines a backward loop with a roll:

  • Gain altitude and approach an object.
  • Pitch backward to loop over the object.
  • Roll to stabilize the drone and complete the maneuver.

Practice this in a simulator before attempting in real life.

Common issues and solutions:
