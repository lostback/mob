Simulator Basics

Master these skills in the simulator before flying your drone.

+

Key Steps:

  1. Use a USB-C cable to connect the Jumper T-Pro to your computer.
  2. Open Liftoff and go to the settings menu to select your controller.
  3. Calibrate the controller in Liftoff to ensure all axes (throttle, yaw, pitch, roll) respond correctly.
+

Key Tips:

  • Choose a beginner-friendly map like an open field or a basic racecourse.
  • Set the simulator to Angle Mode for stable flight during initial training.
  • Adjust the camera tilt to match the Mobula7‚Äôs FPV setup.
+

Hovering is a critical skill to master:

  • Keep the drone stable at a low altitude (1‚Äì2 feet).
  • Use small stick inputs to make precise adjustments.
  • Practice moving forward and backward while maintaining altitude.
+

Throttle controls altitude. Key tips:

  • Maintain a consistent altitude by gently adjusting the throttle stick.
  • Avoid sudden movements to prevent loss of control.
  • Use practice drills to focus on smooth changes in height.
+

Flying patterns helps with directional control:

  • Start with a square pattern: fly forward, turn, fly left, turn, and so on.
  • Progress to figure-eights to practice smooth turns.
  • Keep your movements steady and maintain altitude throughout.
+

Crashes are part of learning! Tips:

  • Use the reset button in the simulator to quickly recover after a crash.
  • Analyze what went wrong and adjust your inputs next time.
  • Focus on improvement, not perfection.
